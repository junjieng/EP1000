Introductions of the Module EP1000

Welcome to EP1000 Digital Fabrications and prototyping This module aims to develop design thinking skills by designing some functional prototypes to meet certain user requiremets. Some of the design thinking skills and technologies includes computer aided design , 2D and 3D modelling Mechanical and electronic design and programming skills.
